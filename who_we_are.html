The SELFLLC Legacy

Sustainable Energy Landscape and Facilities (SELF) is a Ghana-based climate education and renewable energy organization committed to empowering schools, youth, and communities to lead sustainable climate action. Through strategic projects, training programs, and green campus initiatives, SELF bridges the gap between climate knowledge and practical solutions, ensuring that the next generation is equipped to build a resilient and sustainable future.

We work at the intersection of education, renewable energy, environmental stewardship, and climate innovation, delivering practical impact across Ghana and the wider sub-region.
