Education

Climate Smart Schools Program

Build climate literacy and action among students and teachers.

Goal: Build climate literacy and action among students and teachers.

Activities:

  • Train teachers on climate change and renewable energy.
  • Install mini solar systems or gardens for demonstration.
  • Create climate clubs in schools.
  • Run school competitions (climate quiz, clean energy innovation).

Potential Donors: UNICEF, UNESCO, UNDP, Green Climate Fund (GCF), EU Erasmus+.
